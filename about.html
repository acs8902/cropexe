our mission

we created this tool to help students and job applicants in India who struggle with the technical requirements of exam photo and signature submissions. Our goal is to make the process simple, fast, and accessible to everyone.

how it works

this tool processes your images entirely in your browser - no files are uploaded to any server. We use advanced JavaScript libraries to:

  • Crop images to exact exam specifications
  • Automatically compress files to meet size limits
  • Convert between different formats (PDF, HEIC, etc.)
  • Maintain correct aspect ratios and DPI settings
